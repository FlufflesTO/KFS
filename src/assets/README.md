# Industrial Asset Intake

Place optimized, approved local industrial imagery here when final visuals are available.

Required subjects:

- Gas suppression cylinder banks and clean-agent infrastructure.
- Suppression nozzles and protected-room details.
- Server room protection and electrical infrastructure.
- Fire detection control panels, detectors and addressable loop hardware.
- Commissioning procedures, technician testing and maintenance work.
- Control rooms, industrial risers, warehouses and plant spaces.
- Access control, enterprise CCTV and control-room environments only where security is clearly subordinate to fire protection and operational continuity.

Use Astro image optimization for local image assets where practical.

Do not use:

- Residential alarm imagery.
- Security guards.
- Smiling office or call-centre people.
- Handshake stock photos.
- Generic suburban homes.
- Blue-neon hacker visuals.
