Introduction

In accordance with Section 51 of the Promotion of Access to Information Act (No. 2 of 2000), Kharon Fire and Security Solutions (Pty) Ltd makes available this manual outlining the procedures for requesting access to organizational records.

Scope of Records Available

This organization maintains records in accordance with PAIA requirements, including:

  • Organizational policies and procedures
  • Public reports and communications
  • Financial records (subject to exemptions)
  • Employment records (subject to exemptions)

Request Process

To request access to information:

  1. Submit a written request to the Information Officer
  2. Include sufficient detail to identify the requested records
  3. Pay applicable fees as prescribed by regulations
  4. Allow 30 days for processing (may be extended under certain circumstances)
