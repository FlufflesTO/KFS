## 2026-06-08 - Focus Visible Styles for Skip Links
**Learning:** The skip-to-content links in the layout files were missing the strict keyboard focus styles mandated by the design system, causing accessibility issues for keyboard navigators.
**Action:** Applied the standard `focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-kharon-cyan focus-visible:ring-offset-4 focus-visible:ring-offset-kharon-charcoal` classes to all skip-to-content links across the application.
